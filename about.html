About · the first AI FinOps platform

The system of record for AI cost

AI bills are exploding and almost nobody can answer the basic questions: what does my AI cost, where, and am I making money on it? We built the first AI FinOps platform to fix that.

TokenIQ is the first AI FinOps platform — it turns raw token usage into the numbers that matter (cost, carbon and margin) across every model you run. One engine, three products, each lighting up more of the same data as you scale.

Why we exist

Teams are shipping AI features faster than finance can track them. Token prices swing, models multiply, and spend hides across providers and customers. The result: surprise bills, un-priced features, and customers you're quietly losing money on. We believe every company building with AI deserves a clear, trustworthy view of its AI economics — without handing over the contents of its prompts.

One engine, three products

  • Meter — Individuals. Know what your own AI habit costs, with a personal Green Score and a prompt analyzer to spend less.
  • Ledger — Team. Account for every person, project and model your org runs — org-wide spend, budgets and ESG-ready carbon reporting.
  • Margin — Application. For products that resell AI: per-customer cost-of-goods, gross margin and margin-negative alerts. The defensible system of record.

What we believe

  • Trust is the product. We're read-only and metadata-only, and we never store the contents of your prompts or responses. Here's how.
  • Measure first. You can't optimize what you can't see — so measurement is free, forever.
  • Margin compounds. Cost-cutting shrinks as models get cheaper; per-customer margin only gets more important as you grow.
