Pricing FAQ

Common questions

Is it really free to measure?
Yes — analytics and your Green Score are free forever on every product. You only pay when you turn on optimization (Meter, Ledger) or per-customer margin (Margin).
Do I need a credit card to start?
No. Connect a provider and see your dashboard and Green Score with no card and no trial clock.
How is Margin priced?
Margin is usage-based on the spend and number of customers you track, so it scales with the value. It's sales-led — book a demo and we'll map it to your numbers.
Can I change plans later?
Yes — upgrade, downgrade or cancel any time. Paid plans are billed in advance and access continues to the end of the period.
