FAQ

Good questions

Is my data safe?
Yes. tokanalyzr is read-only, your keys are encrypted, and we never store the contents of your prompts or responses — just the usage metadata needed to measure cost and carbon.
Which models does it support?
OpenAI / ChatGPT, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, Llama, Mistral and OpenRouter — with more added regularly.
Do I have to change my code?
No. Connect an API key, point your calls at our optional proxy, or upload a usage export — whichever is easiest.
How accurate is the Green Score?
It combines published energy-per-token benchmarks with regional grid-carbon data — the same method shown above. It's an estimate, but a consistent, comparable one.
Is the free plan really free?
Yes, forever — tracking and your Green Score cost nothing. Pro is $8/mo only when you want the tools that actively cut your spend.
