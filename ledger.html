FAQ

Questions teams ask us

How does it connect to our stack?
Three ways, all read-only: add provider API keys, route through our optional lightweight proxy, or upload usage exports. No code rewrite required.
Is it secure and compliant?
Keys are encrypted, we never store prompt or response content, and we're GDPR-ready with SOC 2 in progress. SSO and budget controls come with Pro.
Will it slow our applications down?
Analytics are passive — zero added latency. The optional routing/caching proxy adds only a few milliseconds and always fails open to your provider.
How is the carbon calculated?
Energy-per-token benchmarks per model × the carbon intensity of the datacenter region it ran in — auditable and exportable for ESG disclosures.
What does it cost?
Analytics and the Green Score are free for your whole team, forever. Pro is $12/user/mo ($9 annual) for optimization and reporting — see plans.
