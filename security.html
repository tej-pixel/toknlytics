Security & Trust

Measure your AI without exposing it

tokanalyzr is built privacy-first: read-only, metadata-only, and architecturally incapable of seeing the contents of your prompts or responses.

Trust is the product. Below is exactly how we handle your data — the same principles across Meter, Ledger and Margin.

👀

Read-only by design

We read usage to measure it — we never write to your systems or alter your AI traffic. Analytics add zero latency; the optional proxy fails open to your provider.

🧾

Metadata only

We process tokens, models, providers, cost and attribution. We never store the contents of your prompts or responses, and never use them to train models.

🔒

Encrypted credentials

Provider API keys are encrypted at rest and in transit, scoped to read usage, and revocable at any time. We don't store full payment card numbers.

👥

Least-privilege access

Access to production data is restricted on a need-to-know basis, logged, and protected with SSO and strong authentication for our team.

How a connection works

You connect in one of three read-only ways, whichever is easiest:

  • Provider API keys — we read usage/billing endpoints only.
  • Optional lightweight proxy — routes calls to compute cost in real time, adding only a few milliseconds and always failing open to your provider.
  • Usage exports — upload provider usage files; nothing live is connected.

Data handling & residency

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We retain usage metadata according to your plan's history window and delete or anonymize account data after closure, subject to legal retention. See our Privacy Policy for full detail and the subprocessor list.

Compliance

  • GDPR / UK GDPR ready — data-subject rights supported; processing documented.
  • SOC 2 — in progress. We're building toward a Type II report and will publish status here.
  • Data Processing Agreement — available for business customers on request.

Some certifications are in progress and described here as roadmap items, not current attestations. We'll update this page as each is completed.

Report a vulnerability

We welcome responsible disclosure. If you believe you've found a security issue, email security@tokanalyzr.ai with details and steps to reproduce. We'll acknowledge promptly and keep you updated. Please don't access data that isn't yours or disrupt the Service while testing.

Questions from your security team? Get in touch — we're happy to complete questionnaires and share documentation under NDA.
