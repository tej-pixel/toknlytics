FAQ

Questions founders & finance ask

How do you know our cost per customer?
Every request you run carries the customer (and member, model, feature and source) that drove it. Margin attributes the real token cost back to that customer — the same way TokenIQ already aggregates spend — so COGS is measured, not estimated.
How do you get what we charge each customer?
Import each customer's plan and price by CSV or API, or sync your billing system (Stripe, Chargebee and more on Enterprise). That revenue is paired with AI COGS to produce gross margin per customer.
How is this different from our billing system?
Billing knows what you charge. It has no idea what each customer costs you in AI. Margin pairs the two — so you see gross margin, not just revenue, on every customer.
Is prompt or response content stored?
No. Margin uses usage metadata only — tokens, model, customer attribution — never the contents of your prompts or responses. Keys are encrypted; we're GDPR-ready with SOC 2 in progress.
What does it cost?
Usage-based on the spend and number of customers you track, so it scales with the value. Book a demo and we'll map it to your numbers.
