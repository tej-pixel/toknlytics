FAQ

Creator program questions

How much can I earn?
You earn recurring commission — 25% to 35% depending on your tier — on every paid subscription you refer, for 12 months per subscriber. Because it recurs, earnings compound as your referrals stack up. Use the calculator for an estimate.
How does the free Premium work?
Once you're approved, we switch on Premium for your own account so you can use and demo the real product. Creators get Meter Pro; Partners and Insiders get full Premium across Ledger and Margin too. It stays free while you're active in the program.
How is a referral tracked?
You get a unique link and code. Anyone who clicks is attributed to you for 90 days; if they subscribe in that window, the commission is yours. You'll see clicks, signups and earnings in your partner dashboard.
When and how do I get paid?
Monthly, via Stripe, once your balance passes $50. You'll connect a payout account when you're approved.
Who gets approved?
Creators with an audience that builds with or buys AI — developers, founders, RevOps/FinOps, technical newsletters and communities. We don't require a minimum follower count, but we do review for fit and brand safety.
Are there rules on how I promote?
Promote honestly, disclose the affiliate relationship as your region requires, and don't bid on our brand terms or spam. Full guidelines are in the partner agreement you'll accept on approval.
