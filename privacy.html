Privacy Policy

We built tokanalyzr.ai to measure AI cost without touching your content. This explains exactly what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Effective date: 13 June 2026 · Last updated: 13 June 2026

tokanalyzr.ai (“tokanalyzr”, “we”, “us”) provides analytics for the cost, usage and environmental impact of AI/LLM usage across our products Meter, Ledger and Margin. This policy applies to our website and those products.

The short version. We are read-only and metadata-only. We measure tokens, models, providers, cost and (for Margin) which customer drove the usage. We never store the contents of your prompts or responses. Your provider API keys are encrypted and used only to read usage. We don't sell your data.

1. What we collect

Account information

Your email address and, for paid plans, billing details handled by our payment processor (we do not store full card numbers).

Usage metadata

To measure cost and carbon we process metadata about your AI calls: timestamps, model and provider, token counts, estimated cost, latency and error status, and the project, environment, feature or member the usage is attributed to. For Margin, this includes the end-customer a request is attributed to and the plan/price you tell us you charge them.

Provider credentials

If you connect a provider via API key or proxy, that credential is encrypted at rest and used only to read usage data. You can revoke it at any time.

What we do not collect

  • The contents of your prompts or model responses.
  • Any data we are not required to in order to compute cost, usage and carbon.

2. How we use it

  • Provide the service: dashboards, Green Score, budgets, per-customer margin and alerts.
  • Operate, secure and improve the product.
  • Communicate with you about your account, security and (with your consent) product updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use the contents of prompts or responses to train models.

3. Legal bases (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Where the GDPR applies, we rely on: performance of a contract (to provide the service you signed up for), legitimate interests (to secure and improve the product), consent (for optional marketing), and legal obligation where required. You can withdraw consent at any time.

4. Sharing & subprocessors

We share data only with service providers that help us run tokanalyzr (e.g. cloud hosting, payment processing, email and error monitoring), each bound by contract to protect it. We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights. A current list of subprocessors is available on request at privacy@tokanalyzr.ai.

5. Retention

We keep account data for as long as your account is active and usage metadata according to your plan's history window. When you close your account we delete or anonymize your data within a commercially reasonable period, except where we must retain it for legal or accounting reasons.

6. Security

Credentials are encrypted, access is restricted on a need-to-know basis, and we never persist prompt or response content. See our Security & Trust page for details. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work hard to protect your data and will notify you of incidents as required by law.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, restrict or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, email privacy@tokanalyzr.ai. You also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority.

8. Cookies

Our marketing site uses only essential cookies needed to function. If we add analytics or preference cookies, we'll ask for consent where required and update this policy.

9. Contact

tokanalyzr.ai is operated by [Legal entity name], [registered address]. Questions about this policy or your data? Email privacy@tokanalyzr.ai.

When we make material changes to this policy we'll update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you.
